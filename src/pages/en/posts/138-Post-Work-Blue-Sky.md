## Technical Related

**Python for Data Analysis (3rd Edition)**  
<https://wesmckinney.com/book/>  
The third edition of this classic remains the definitive guide to manipulating, processing, cleaning, and analyzing data in Python. perfect for aspiring data analysts.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/td/dzf4wq.png" width="800" />

**Illustrated CS Fundamentals by Xiaolin**  
<https://xiaolincoding.com/>  
I used to think these "illustrated" guides were just for quick exam prep, but Xiaolin’s work is actually incredibly thorough. it’s a grounded way to master the essentials of computer science for those from non-CS backgrounds.

**Rendering PDFs with JavaScript and Canvas**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/RGRbgcNveAcqXnRNKt_0Jw>  
A deep dive into using JavaScript’s binary operations to parse and render PDF content directly in the browser.
