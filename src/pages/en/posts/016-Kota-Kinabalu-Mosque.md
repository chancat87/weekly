## Good Reads and Learning

**Decoding 2020 Frontend Technology Trends**  
<https://juejin.cn/post/6908713513271689224>  
Tencent's IMWeb team reviews seven major trends: TS, React, WASM, Low-Code, Serverless, R&D Efficiency, and WebRTC.

**Vite: A Silky-Smooth Developer Experience**  
<https://juejin.cn/post/6906674140792094727>  
A very detailed explanation of Vite’s underlying principles. Well worth a read.

**Cocos: The "Catfish" Stirring Up the H5 Game World**  
<https://jelly.jd.com/article/5fbe616a7482df01463e27e3>  
A narrative-style account from JD developers on building H5 games with Cocos. It’s as engaging as a novel.

**MOIVA: A "Comparison Shopping" Tool for JS Libraries**  
<https://moiva.io/?compare=react+svelte+vue>
