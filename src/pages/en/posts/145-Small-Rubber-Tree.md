## Technical Related

**Deep Dive into Architecture Principles and Practice**  
<https://thebyte.com.cn/>  
A thorough online book that introduces the entire architecture ecosystem—covering underlay networking, containers, cloud-native storage, gateways, microservices, and cost management. perfect for engineers wanting a big-picture view.

**Way to AGI**  
<https://waytoagi.feishu.cn/wiki/QPe5w5g7UisbEkkow8XcDmOpn8e>  
A comprehensive and easy-to-understand AI learning path. it covers everything from basic concepts to advanced applications. bookmark this for your spare time.

## Trending Tools

**BookStash: Decide if a book is worth your time in 3 minutes**  
<https://bookstash.io/>  
If you're a book lover, check out BookStash. it provides 3-minute summaries of books recommended by famous figures, helping you decide whether to buy the full version. many are classics with available translations.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/1YmroJ.png"   width="800" />

**Track Awesome List Updates Daily**  
<https://trackawesomelist.com/>  
I love the "Awesome" lists on GitHub, but I don't want to check them daily for changes. this tool tracks over 500 Awesome repos and summarizes the latest updates for you—it even supports RSS!  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/2fiMnU.png"   width="800" />

**engblogs: Technical Blog Aggregator**  
<https://engineeringblogs.xyz/>  
A great tool that aggregates RSS feeds from over 500 engineering blogs, keeping you updated with the latest posts from top companies and individual engineers.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/zktGKk.png"   width="800" />

**useAnimations: A Collection of Animated Icons**  
<https://useanimations.com/index.html>  
Adding micro-animations can really make your website "pop." this collection of icons uses Lottie (JSON + SVG), giving you high-quality, smooth animations that are easy to implement.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/3yAaFK.png"   width="800" />

**SPIKE.NEWS: A Personalized News Feed for Engineers**  
<https://spike.news/>  
A curated feed for developers that aggregates content from Hacker News, Dev Community, Smashing Magazine, and Reddit into a single readable interface.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/bs9TRQ.png"   width="800" />
