## Technical Learning

<https://tw93.fun/2024-01-12/open.html>  
I finally compiled the notes from my "Open Source Growth Journey" talk at GitHub Universe 2023 in Shanghai. I talk about my 9 years of open-source projects starting from university and my experiences in building successful open-source products.

<https://zcool.com.cn/work/ZNjczMDA1NzY=.html>
