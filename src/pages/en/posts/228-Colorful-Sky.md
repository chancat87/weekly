## Trending Tools

**Monitor your network traffic comfortably with Sniffnet**  
<https://github.com/GyulyVGC/sniffnet>  
A network monitoring tool that helps you easily track internet traffic. Whether you want to gather statistics, or you're curious about whom you're exchanging data with, or need to deeply inspect what's happening in your network, it should meet your needs.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/RYYT3522.png" width="800" />

**Drawnix: Open-source whiteboard tool**  
<https://drawnix.com>  
This open-source whiteboard tool, Drawnix, is quite concise. It supports casual drawing, mind maps, and workflow diagrams. You can deploy it yourself for your own use.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/SCR-20250629-tuwk21.jpeg" width="800" />

**Keycheck: Shortcut lookup tool**  
<https://keycheck.dev/>  
Setting shortcuts for your software can be challenging. The best practice is to maintain high consistency with other tools to minimize user learning cost. It also helps avoid conflicts with system shortcuts. This tool lets you find the right shortcuts for your APP in seconds.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/wCjSXW21.png" width="800" />
