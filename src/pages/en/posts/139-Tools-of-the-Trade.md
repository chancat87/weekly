## Trending Tools

**Talk: Open-Source P2P Video Calling**  
<https://tlk.li/>  
A free, open-source, and peer-to-peer web video call tool. No registration or downloads required—just generate a random room link and share it.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/2z/6JJ7FS.png" width=800 />

**CodeGeeX2: Self-Host Your Own AI Code Model**  
<https://github.com/THUDM/CodeGeeX2>  
If you can't use GitHub Copilot due to company security policies, check out CodeGeeX2 from Tsinghua. it’s based on ChatGLM2-6B and is trained on massive amounts of code, making it a powerful self-hosted alternative.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/ls/hl4ml8.png"   width="800" />

**Mackup: Back Up Your Mac Settings**  
<https://github.com/lra/mackup/>  
An essential tool for Mac users. it backs up almost all your app configurations and settings to a central storage of your choice, making it a breeze to set up a new machine.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/z6/0apG1O.png"   width="800" />

**LocationSimulator: Mock Your GPS on iOS**  
<https://github.com/Schlaubischlump/LocationSimulator>  
A handy tool for testing location-based features without jailbreaking. it lets you mock locations on iOS, iPadOS, and the Xcode simulator.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/yt/NR6Zuj.png"   width="800" />
