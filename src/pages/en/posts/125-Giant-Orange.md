## Technical Related

**The Game Engine that Shook the World (Video)**  
<https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1Hk4y1q7Rz/>  
A brilliant video from Bilibili that explains complex game engine concepts—like global illumination, distance fields, and Lumen—in an accessible way. as a former graphics novice, I found it incredibly inspiring.

**YouTube: Analysis of ChatGPT Principles**  
<https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yiY4nPOzJEg>  
A great breakdown of how ChatGPT works by Professor Hung-yi Lee. he has a fantastic channel full of modern machine learning courses explained in simple terms.

**Concurrent Chunk Downloads via HTTP Range**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/kZvD2OvsY-aFfb34grjqug>

## Trending Tools

**LLamaChat: Run LLMs Locally on Mac**  
<https://llamachat.app/>  
A user-friendly Mac tool that lets you chat with models like LLama, Alpaca, and GPT4All locally. it removes all the complex configuration steps, making it perfect for non-technical users to explore local AI.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/55/TinySnap-2023-04-12-21.58.57.png" width="800" />

**Glasp: Social Web Highlighter & AI Summary**  
<https://glasp.co/home>  
A powerful browser extension for highlighting and annotating content on Twitter, YouTube, and the web. it uses AI to summarize your highlights and lets you see what others are reading and annotating, which is a great way to discover high-quality content.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/qc/TinySnap-2023-04-09-11.18.04.png" width="800" />

**ifixit: The Hardcore Repair Guide**  
<https://zh.ifixit.com/Guide>  
The definitive site for learning how to fix your tech. whether it’s a Mac, iPhone, or even your car, ifixit provides step-by-step guides with detailed screenshots. a word of warning: only attempt if you’re confident in your manual skills!  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/dx/TinySnap-2023-04-09-11.43.23.png" width="800" />

**CodeRadio: Background Music for Coding**  
<https://coderadio.freecodecamp.org/>  
A 24/7 lofi and electronic music stream curated by freeCodeCamp. it's minimalist and distraction-free—perfect for staying in the flow state while programming or reading.

## Just Looking Around

**Design Patterns from the Ant Design Team**  
<https://www.yuque.com/ant-design/design-pattern>  
A very grounded exploration of common design patterns for lists, inputs, and interactions. it explains the "what," "why," and "when" behind modern UI decisions.

**Learn Prompting Course**  
<https://learnprompting.org/zh-Hans/docs/intro>  
As "Prompt Engineering" becomes a real job, this course provides a serious, in-depth look at how to communicate effectively with AI to get the best results.

**The Vanishing Degree (Huxiu Article)**  
A look at why many universities are axing traditional majors like Public Administration and Business Management, and the shift in how young graduates are viewing their career paths.
