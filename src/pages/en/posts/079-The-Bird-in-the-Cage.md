<https://blog.jim-nielsen.com/2022/inspecting-web-views-in-macos/>  
Did you know you could do this? a very handy trick for debugging web content running inside native Mac applications.

**EPUB.js: E-book Rendering in the Browser**  
<http://futurepress.org/>  
A powerful library for typesetting and displaying EPUB books directly on the web. the layout results are very professional.

<https://github.com/farzher/fuzzysort>

**PlayCover: Run iOS Games on your Mac**
