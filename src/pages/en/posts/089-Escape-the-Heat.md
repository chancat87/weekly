## Front-End Technology

**What Problems does Web3.0 Actually Solve?**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/-yWw1qJ2hcpno62ibmtJCQ>  
A critical look at the space: which scenarios *require* Web3 and cannot be done in Web2? how many people actually need those products, and are they genuinely more efficient?

**Optimizing Long Lists in Mini Programs**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/zgpK6L0Tf81KIhf-4k-gnA>  
Long-list performance remains one of the trickiest problems in cross-platform development. reading this made me nostalgic for the early days of Weex.

**Post-Mortem: The 2021.07.13 Bilibili Outage**  
<https://www.bilibili.com/read/cv17521097>  
It’s rare and refreshing to see a company publish a detailed, transparent post-mortem of a major failure on their technical blog. props to the Bilibili team for the honesty.

**Annotated Linux 0.11 Source Code**  
<https://github.com/sunym1993/flash-linux0.11-talk>  
If you have some free time, try reading through this as if it were a novel. it’s a fascinating insight into the core logic of an OS.

<https://github.com/tw93/MiaoYan/releases>  
The big 1.0 release is out, featuring a new mode that lets you turn your Markdown directly into beautiful slide presentations. give it a spin!
