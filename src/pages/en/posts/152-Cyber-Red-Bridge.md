## Technical Learning

**Dilemma and Solutions for the New Generation of Frontend Devs**  
<https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/664946599>  
A talk for developers who started after the React/Vue era. in an age where the "wheel-making" era has passed and competition is fierce, how should an engineer grow? Focus on solving real business problems and becoming a "product engineer" rather than just a coder.

**How to be a Programmer (Chinese Version)**  
<https://braydie.gitbooks.io/how-to-be-a-programmer/content/zh/>  
A Chinese translation of the famous guide on managing teams, personal skills, and navigating a career path.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/o3/SCR-20231029-tnbx.png" width="800" />

**Tsinghua CS Course Strategy**  
<https://rekcarc-tsc-uht.readthedocs.io/en/latest/index.html>  
An open-source collection of notes and guides for computer science students, following the curriculum of Tsinghua University.

## Trending Tools

**MUSCLEWIKI: Exercise Guide**  
<https://musclewiki.com/>  
A visual guide where you click a muscle to see how to train it. perfect for fitness enthusiasts.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/a5/SCR-20231105-tbgi.png" width="800" />

**Squoosh: Modern Image Compression**  
<https://squoosh.app/>  
A fast, beautiful, and intuitive image compression tool from Google. much better than older alternatives.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/84/SCR-20231105-tdnf.png" width="800" />

**MDNice: Markdown Editor for WeChat/Zhihu**

Convert Markdown into perfectly formatted posts for platforms like WeChat or Zhihu. I personally use the "Geek Black" theme.
