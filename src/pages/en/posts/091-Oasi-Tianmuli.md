## Front-End Technology

**How to Write "Irreplaceable" Code**  
<https://juejin.cn/post/7126888773647876110>  
A hilarious look at code anti-patterns. for educational purposes only, of course—write this way and you might be "irreplaceable," but you also won't have any friends left on the team!

**js1024: The Annual JavaScript Golfing Contest**  
<https://js1024.fun/results/2022>  
The results for the latest JS code-golfing contest are out. the level of complexity developers can pack into just 1,024 bytes is always staggering.

**A Stunning Visual Web Experiment**  
<https://camera-webgi.vercel.app/>  
An incredible demonstration of what’s possible with modern web graphics. definitely check out the source to see how they handled the lighting and textures.

## Trending Open Source

**gum: Glamorous Command Line Scripts**  
<https://github.com/charmbracelet/gum>  
A tool for building beautiful, interactive shell scripts. it’s incredibly stylish and a great way to make your internal tools feel premium.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/ku/demo.gif" width="600px"/>

**Emoji Mixer: Combine any Two Emojis**  
<https://tikolu.net/emojimix/%F0%9F%8D%8A+%F0%9F%A4%AF>  
A fun little site for mashing up different emojis. the results are surprisingly seamless.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/r2/82z1kl.jpg" width="800" />

**Arctype: Modern Database Management**  
<https://arctype.com/>  
A fast and collaborative SQL client that’s much more user-friendly than traditional alternatives.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/9h/PKbDK4.jpg" width="800" />

**qnm: Query node_modules like a Pro**  
<https://github.com/ranyitz/qnm>  
A handy CLI for inspecting your `node_modules` directory, checking versions, and finding duplicates.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/1p/o4brxx.jpg" width="800" />

## Just Looking Around

**The Story of Yuque and DingTalk**  
<https://www.xiaoyuzhoufm.com/episode/62ed2b1d226f5c1fa0d58357>  
A fascinating podcast episode on the history of Yuque, its relationship with Alipay, and why it wasn't absorbed by DingTalk. it’s one of the few long-form podcasts I’ve finished recently.

**The Past, Present, and Future of Chinese Podcasting**  
<https://sspai.com/post/74874>  
A deep dive into the evolution of the podcast medium in China. if you’re looking to get into podcasting, this is an excellent primer.

**The Figures Who Shaped Automotive History**  
A zhihu discussion on the great pioneers of the car industry. I’d argue that Elon Musk is definitely one of the most significant figures of our current era.

**The Massive Scale of "Material Science" Paper Fraud**  
A sobering report on how 800 fraudulent research papers were manufactured. it’s a stark reminder of the "cat and mouse" game between institutional policy and deceptive practices.
