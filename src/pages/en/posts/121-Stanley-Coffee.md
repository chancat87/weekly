## Trending Tools

**Samantha: Your AI English Coach**  
<https://t.me/samantha_x64_bot>  
This Telegram bot feels exactly like the Samantha from the movie *Her*. it’s an incredible, low-cost way to practice your English speaking—she’s surprisingly clever and her accent is impeccable.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/9z/K3gYle.jpg" width="800" />

**My 90s TV: Nostalgic Channel Surfing**  
<https://my90stv.com/>  
A brilliant interactive site that mocks a 90s TV set. browse various channels to see music videos, commercials, and shows from that era. it’s a portal back to a different time.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/3z/TinySnap-2023-03-18-15.57.09.png" width="800" />

**Lama Cleaner: Open-Source AI Object Removal**  
<https://huggingface.co/spaces/Sanster/Lama-Cleaner-lama>  
An AI-powered tool to remove unwanted objects or people from your photos. it’s free, open-source, and arguably more powerful than many paid alternatives.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/ss/TinySnap-2023-03-12-23.16.02.png" width="800" />

**Catppuccin: Soft Themes for Everything**  
<https://github.com/catppuccin/catppuccin>  
A collection of "low-contrast" themes available for almost every developer tool and app. perfect for those who want a workspace that’s easy on the eyes.
