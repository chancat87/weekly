## Front-End Technology

**A Guide to Sketch Plugin Development**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/NLyCKEuTEiVMXMrHVDcvHQ>  
A foundational introduction for frontend developers interested in building tools for Sketch.

**Meituan's Framework for Containerless Terminal Development**  
<https://tech.meituan.com/2021/11/11/meituan-waimai-containerless-framework.html>  
A look into the reasoning process behind their architectural shifts. Meituan’s tech team definitely loves a good complex architectural diagram.

**Practicing Integrated Rich Text Management Across Platforms**  
<https://tech.meituan.com/2021/11/25/page-webide-thrift.html>  
Insights into how Meituan handles rich text consistency across different platforms. The scope is ambitious, though it still feels like it’s in the early stages.

**Bundling Your Node.js Express App with esbuild**  
<https://devtails.medium.com/bundling-your-node-js-express-app-with-esbuild-5aecc36c5047>  
A simple case study on how to leverage the speed of esbuild for Node.js Express applications.

**Fleet: JetBrains’ Answer to VS Code**  
<https://blog.jetbrains.com/zh-hans/blog/2021/11/29/fleet/>  
This looks incredible. Currently in preview, it’s a massive shift for JetBrains towards a lightweight, high-performance editor.

**Automa: An Extension for Browser Automation**  
<https://github.com/Kholid060/automa>

**CasaOS: A Slick Open Source Home Cloud System**
