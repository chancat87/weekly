## Trending Open Source

**SigmaOS 1.0: The Space-Based Browser**  
<https://sigmaos.com/>  
A new browser that focuses on categories, spaces, and a "card-based" workflow even more than Arc does. definitely worth trying if you love exploring new productivity tools.
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/5sgHad.gif" width="800" />

**HyperUI: Polished Tailwind CSS Components**  
<https://www.hyperui.dev/>  
I highly recommend Tailwind CSS for its simplicity and speed. HyperUI takes it further by providing beautifully designed components that you can drop into any project with just a copy-paste.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/3r/4.gif" width="800" />

**DEVICES.CSS: Apple Devices in Pure CSS**  
<https://devicescss.xyz/>  
A fun library that lets you render realistic Apple devices (iPhones, Macs, etc.) using only CSS—no images required.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/zxi2eJ.png" width="800" />

**RegexLearn: Gamified Regex Learning**  
<https://regexlearn.com/zh-cn>  
Regular expressions are a vital skill for any engineer, yet few truly master them. this tool makes learning regex fun and interactive.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/g3/7UptTR.png" width="800" />

**ridermansb: The npx Resume**  
<https://github.com/Ridermansb/ridermansb>  
A fun way to present your professional info—just run `npx ridermansb` in the terminal to see the author's bio and contact details.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/sn/MEYCoT.png" width="800" />
