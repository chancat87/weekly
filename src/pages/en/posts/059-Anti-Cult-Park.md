## Front-End Technology

**What Exactly is Web 3.0?**  
<https://www.zhihu.com/question/341559382>  
Web3 is everywhere lately. What is it? How does it relate to blockchain? You can also check out [Ruan Yifeng’s take](http://www.ruanyifeng.com/blog/2021/12/web3.html) on it.

**How to Do Front-End Unit Testing**  
<https://juejin.cn/post/7039108357554176037>  
An excellent guide that covers everything from reasoning to implementation across various scenarios.

**My Year of Contributing to the Vue.js Ecosystem**  
<https://zhuanlan.zhihu.com/p/441465938>  
A great read for anyone looking to get started in open source. These contribution habits can be just as valuable when applied to your day job.

**Analyzing Dependency Management in npm, Yarn, and pnpm**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/3k4u-jw_iKsBeYyHJoSKMA>  
A comparison of the major frontend package managers. Spoiler: go use pnpm.

**Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis in Food Delivery**  
<https://tech.meituan.com/2021/12/09/meituan-aspect-based-sentiment-analysis-daodian.html>  
A look at how Meituan processes and learns from the millions of user reviews on platforms like Dianping.

## Trending Open Source

**GFPGAN: Restore Old Photos to HD**  
<https://github.com/TencentARC/GFPGAN>  
This is impressive. Restoring old photos used to be a costly professional service, and now AI is making it accessible to everyone.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/vHFsTE.jpg" width="800" />

**Tabby: A More Modern Terminal**  
<https://github.com/Eugeny/tabby>  
Worth a try if you’re looking for something different from the traditional CLI experience, though I personally still prefer Hyper’s aesthetics.

**SwiftPamphletApp: A Living Swift Manual**

## Just Looking Around

**China's Greatest Future Challenge**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/eS2i1gY4mdNiM_0ugpkxbA>  
A discussion on how a pragmatism-heavy culture might be impacting the quality and quantity of scientific talent, and the long-term challenges of fostering true scientific innovation.

**Bill Gates: After a Tough Year, I'm Still Full of Optimism**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/X8QI1zaligHxBcWB-Rrd8A>
