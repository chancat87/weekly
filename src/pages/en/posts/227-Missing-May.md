**Apple iOS 26 Library Sketch Design File**  
<https://sketch.com/s/f63aa308-1f82-498c-8019-530f3b846db9>  
The Sketch design library for Apple iOS 26 might be useful for those designing new software. Apple's "multi-device consistency" in the VR era—while I feel their designs aren't always great at first, I usually get used to them after a while.

**Visualization of Subway Lines in Chinese Cities**  
<https://subway.cuvii.dev>  
A fun tool for map enthusiasts: visualizations of metro lines in Chinese cities. Hangzhou has more lines than I thought. You can choose a city and line to see detailed station routes.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/v2STGN22.png" width="800" />

**LibreTube: Alternative YouTube Client for Android**  
<https://github.com/libre-tube/LibreTube>  
If you use Android, try this third-party YouTube client; the interface is quite beautiful.
