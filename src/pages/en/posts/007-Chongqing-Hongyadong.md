## Open Source and Tools

**Auto.js: JavaScript Automation for Android**  
<https://github.com/hyb1996/Auto.js>

**FingerprintJS: Modern Browser Fingerprinting**  
<https://github.com/fingerprintjs/fingerprintjs>
