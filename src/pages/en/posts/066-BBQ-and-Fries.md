## Trending Open Source

**libpag: The Tencent PAG Animation Workflow**  
<https://github.com/Tencent/libpag/blob/main/README.zh_CN.md>  
A comprehensive solution from AE plugins to cross-platform rendering SDKs. it’s a powerful alternative to Lottie for high-fidelity animations.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/dg/640.gif" width="800" />

**Tauri: Tiny, Blazing Fast App Packaging**  
<https://github.com/tauri-apps/tauri>  
A major alternative to Electron. by using the underlying OS webview and a Rust backend instead of bundling Chromium and Node.js, Tauris can produce apps that are only a few megabytes in size.

**Stay: A Native iOS Safari Extension Manager**  
<https://github.com/shenruisi/Stay>  
Finally, you can run custom "Tampermonkey" style scripts natively in Safari on your iPhone. love this level of control.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/9u/jGpnZu.png" width="800" />

**Logseq: A Knowledge Management Hub**  
<https://github.com/logseq/logseq>  
A powerful Markdown-based local notebook that helps you visualize and analyze the connections between your notes.
