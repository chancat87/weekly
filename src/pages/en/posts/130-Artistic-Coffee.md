**Ogden’s Basic English: 850 Words for Everything**  
<http://ogden.basic-english.org/>  
The author of "Basic English" discovered that 90% of the concepts in the Oxford Dictionary can be expressed using just 850 words. the goal is to simplify communication while maintaining perfect clarity. A very interesting approach to linguistics.
