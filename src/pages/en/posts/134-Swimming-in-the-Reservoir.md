## Trending Tools

**AI Bot Navigation**  
<https://ai-bot.cn/>  
Probably the most comprehensive and clearly organized AI tool directory I've seen. it covers everything—chatbots, translation, coding, writing, images, audio, video, and more.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/zd/aC6rEu.png" width="800" />

**Splinespiration: 3D Spline Gallery**  
<https://splinespiration.com/#gallery>  
A stunning collection of 3D Spline creations. Unlike other showcase sites, it often provides the source files so you can jump in and start editing immediately (for educational purposes).  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/pz/NXcQmu.png" width="800" />

**tabliss: A Minimalist New Tab Page**  
<https://tabliss.io/>  
I’ve switched to tabliss for my browser’s new tab page. it’s lightweight, free of clutter, and lets you display gorgeous wallpapers from various categories like film, art, and nature.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/c8/Ql3jOK.png" width="800" />

**Jellylade: Beautiful Screenshot Tool**  
<https://app.jellylade.com/>  
A fantastic alternative to paid screenshot plugins. it’s completely free and produces high-quality, textured screenshots that look incredibly professional.
