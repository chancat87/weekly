## Trending Open Source

**Shottr: A Fast and Lightweight Screenshot Tool for Mac**  
<https://shottr.cc/>  
I used iShot for a long time, but as it moved to a subscription model and became more bloated, I went looking for an alternative. Shottr is incredibly fast, supports long screenshots and annotations, and feels just like the classic tools of a few years ago.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/9l/qfJ9gp.jpg" width="800" />

**PicView: The Lightest Photo Viewer for macOS**  
<https://wl879.github.io/apps/picview/index.html>  
Honestly, this is probably the best and most lightweight image viewer I’ve found for the Mac. it just works perfectly.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/63/uvKfnO.jpg" width="800" />

**UnoCSS: The Instant Atomic CSS Engine**  
<https://github.com/unocss/unocss>  
Think of it as a highly optimized, on-demand version of Tailwind CSS. it’s incredibly fast, and I’m curious to see if it becomes the new standard for performance-focused builds.

**TeslaMate: A Self-Hosted Data Logger for your Tesla**  
<https://github.com/adriankumpf/teslamate>  
A powerful dashboard for tracking everything your car is doing, all hosted on your own infrastructure.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/po/zPMv1c.jpg" width="800" />

**Reason: Type-Safe Programming for JS and OCaml**  
<https://reasonml.github.io/zh-CN/>  
Reason lets you write simple, fast, and robust type-safe code while leveraging the massive ecosystems of both JavaScript and OCaml.
