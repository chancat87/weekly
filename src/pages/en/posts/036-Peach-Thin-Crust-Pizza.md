## Front-End Technology

**Inside the Notion Editor**  
<https://juejin.cn/post/6975786088006696990>  
Even if you find Notion a bit too complex for daily use, the engineering behind its block-based editor is fascinating and worth studying.

**Generating Stroke Order Animations for Chinese Characters**  
<https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/dhh5UVXPD9d9bD3JfYOU9w>  
A technical look at how to take any font file and dynamically generate the corresponding animated stroke sequence data.

**Three Approaches to VR Property Viewing in the Browser**  
<https://juejin.cn/post/6973865268426571784>  
A summary of the current landscape for building 360-degree interactive housing tours with frontend tech.

**Intelligent Compute Allocation for Meituan Waimai Ads**  
<https://tech.meituan.com/2021/06/17/waimai-ai-advertisement.html>  
Food delivery traffic peaks sharply during lunch and dinner. This article explores how Meituan dynamically allocates computational resources to ensure efficiency during these high-demand "twin peaks."
