## Trending Tools

**Corey Chiu's blog theme: Perfect for engineers with portfolios**  
<https://coreychiu.com/>  
This blog theme is perfect for engineers who have work to show. it does a great job displaying social media, projects, open-source work, recent thoughts, and blog posts with a great sense of aesthetics.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/bSrwrp20.png" width="800" />

**NotionNext: Website builder based on Notion notes**  
<https://github.com/tangly1024/NotionNext>  
If you use Notion and aren't keen on building a personal site from scratch, try NotionNext. It's open-source, free, and provides complete tutorials. You could use it as a base and further optimize it using Cursor.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/SCR-20250309-resg20.jpeg" width="800" />

**Pagefind: Add search functionality to your blog or documentation site**  
<https://pagefind.app/>  
Pagefind is a fully static search library that doesn't rely on any infrastructure. It's what powers the search for Trending Weekly—easy to configure and very convenient.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/ektHvp20.png" width="800" />

**WXT: Browser extension development framework**  
<https://wxt.dev/>  
If you're looking to develop browser extensions, try WXT. It's a comprehensive framework supporting multiple browsers and frameworks, providing toolsets to help you develop faster. I plan to try it next time I develop one.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/SCR-20250309-rhnd20.jpeg" width="800" />

**Beautiful themes for Web video and audio players**  
<https://player.style/>  
This is interesting: themes for your web video and audio players. If you want a different experience from default player components—like simulating YouTube or other popular app styles—check this out and just copy the code.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/uPic/SCR-20250309-rlrw20.jpeg" width="800" />
