## Development Technology

**Internationalization with Pure CSS**  
<https://codepen.io/indieboy86/pen/kKOzoQ>  
A brilliant and lightweight approach found on CodePen. it shows how you can handle basic i18n using only CSS, keeping your HTML clean and simple.

**First-Party Cookie Recipes**  
<https://web.dev/first-party-cookie-recipes/>  
A guide from the Chrome team on how to set up secure, cross-browser compatible first-party cookies as third-party cookies are phased out.

**You May Not Need a Bundler for your NPM Library**  
<https://cmdcolin.github.io/posts/2022-05-27-youmaynotneedabundler>  
A must-read for anyone publishing their first package. it looks at how some of the most popular libraries handle their distribution without over-complicating things.

## Trending Open Source

**MiaoYan: A Swift-Powered Markdown Notebook for Mac**  
<https://miaoyan.vercel.app/>  
A native macOS app built with Swift. if you’re a fan of local Markdown editors and prefer the speed of native code over Electron-based apps, you’ll love this.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/zs/0XypF5.jpg" width="800" />

**SwiftBar: Customize your Mac Menu Bar with Scripts**  
<https://github.com/swiftbar/SwiftBar>  
An incredibly powerful tool that lets you display information from any script directly in your system menu bar. the possibilities for customization are endless.

**Openbase: Choose the Right Library for your Project**  
<https://openbase.com/>  
A platform that provides detailed metrics on package stability, download trends, and community feedback to help you make informed technical choices.

**Kratos: Cloud-Native Identity and User Management**  
<https://github.com/ory/kratos>  
An interesting take on a "headless" identity system that eliminates the need for users to repeatedly log in across different services.
