## Technical Learning

**State of AI Report 2023**  
<https://tw93.fun/images/pdf/2023AI.pdf>  
A translated version (by Tencent) of the massive 2023 AI State Report. it’s high-density content that shows exactly how AI has evolved this year and where the gaps lie.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/nb/SCR-20231106-qqyv.png" width="800" />

**How To Be A Programmer (Community Version)**  
<https://github.com/braydie/HowToBeAProgrammer>  
This guide covers everything from beginner to intermediate and advanced levels of programming. pair it with the Chinese version for a complete look.

**System Design 101**  
<https://github.com/ByteByteGoHq/system-design-101>  
A GitHub repository that uses diagrams and simple terms to explain complex systems—covering protocols, architecture patterns, databases, caching, microservices, and security. Perfect for anyone interested in backend architecture.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/kd/SCR-20231105-oyog.png" width="800" />

## Trending Tools

**Meiye: A Designer Portfolio Hub**  
<https://meiye.art/>  
One of the best design portals in China. it includes inspiration, UI, illustrations, screenshots, logos, fonts, and even AI prompts. very polished interface.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/jz/SCR-20231112-seav.png" width="800" />

**ASCII Art Collection**  
<https://asciiart.website/index.php?art=animals/aardvarks>  
This repository is nearly 30 years old! back when Netscape 1.0 was the king, people were already creating huge collections of ASCII art. use it to add some flavor to your code.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/dr/SCR-20231105-pqyz.png" width="800" />

**App Motion Design Gallery**  
<https://appmotion.design/>  
A curated collection of excellent app motion and animation designs.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/y2/SCR-20231112-sese.png" width="800" />

**App Store Screenshots Club**  
<https://scrnshts.club/>  
A comprehensive archive of App Store screenshots for design inspiration.
