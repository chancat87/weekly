## Trending Tools

**Alacritty: The "World's Fastest" Terminal**  
<https://github.com/alacritty/alacritty>  
I recently switched from Hyper to Alacritty. it’s quite plain out of the box, but the performance is staggering. after some weekend tweaking, I’ve got it looking just the way I want—I’ll be writing a separate post on how I set it up.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/3x/PKG7Li.png" width="700" />

**IT Tools: The Developer’s Swiss Army Knife**  
<https://it-tools.tech/>  
A beautifully designed collection of online utilities for developers. it covers everything from encryption and unit conversion to image and math tools.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/ch/IyZOnm.jpg" width="700" />

**Write & Improve: British Council Writing Tool**  
<https://writeandimprove.com/>  
A free tool from Cambridge University for improving English writing. you can write on hundreds of topics and get instant feedback and level certification. perfect for anyone looking to refine their written expression.  
<img src="https://gw.alipayobjects.com/zos/k/g8/DzIjaS.jpg" width="700" />

**MyFakeInfo: Generate Test Personas**  
<https://www.myfakeinfo.com/>  
A tool for generating dummy data like US social security cards, IDs, and credit card numbers for testing purposes. it’s all procedurally generated and has no legal risk—just don’t use it for anything malicious!
