## Trending Tools

**EpicEasing: Ease Animation Debugger and Previewer**  
<https://epiceasing.com/>  
A polished tool for generating and debugging easing animations. it supports Ease, Spring, and Bounce curves and can export code for CSS, SCSS, Objective-C, and Swift.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/Sap22r.png"   width="800" />

**Compiling Chromium from Source**  
<https://chromium.googlesource.com/chromium/src/+/main/docs/mac_build_instructions.md>  
I spent a few hours on Friday evening compiling Chromium. it’s a fascinating process—if you have some time and a powerful Mac, it’s worth a try.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/KsAdZ0.png"   width="800" />

**RealChar - Your Realtime AI Character**  
<https://github.com/Shaunwei/RealChar>  
Create, customize, and converse with your own AI character or companion in real-time. You can define their personality, background, and even their voice.  
<img src="https://cdn.fliggy.com/upic/dtz8sy.png"   width="800" />

**The Pudding: Visual Journalism**  
<https://pudding.cool>  
A highly creative site that combines data visualization with storytelling to explain complex topics. it’s a brilliant example of modern multimedia journalism.

**Sam Altman’s Advice for Ambitious 19-Year-Olds**  
<https://blog.samaltman.com/advice-for-ambitious-19-year-olds>  
The short version:

1. Work with smart people.
2. Build something valuable.
3. Take appropriate risks.
4. If you go to school, make sure you learn something.
5. Join high-growth startups over big companies.
6. Don't let salary be the primary factor.
7. Only start a company if you absolutely love the idea.
